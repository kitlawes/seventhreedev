History Line

History Line is a collaborative learning game for the iPad focusing on US History. The game can be played by 1-6 players. As a single player game, a series of historical events are presented on the screen and the goal of the game is to place the events onto a timeline at the bottom of the screen in the correct sequence. In multi-player mode, up to 6 people can play by connecting their iPads via Bluetooth.

Puzzlement

Puzzlement (*Some Assembly Required) is a multi-player jigsaw puzzle game for the iPad. Choose from a library of puzzles including a brand new Puzzle of the Day every morning. Partner with your friends on Game Center to complete puzzles as a team, or let Game Center auto-match you with other players around the world.
