OnFoot - Fitness as Currency


OnFoot is a health and fitness tracking app that allows users to earn FitCredits by walking, running, cycling and visiting gyms or by scoring data contributed by other fitness apps to HealthKit, Apple’s collaborative database for health and fitness data. With OnFoot, users can customize their fitness experience. No matter what a user’s activity of choice, they can use their favorite apps and still earn FitCredits. Once earned, FitCredits can be used for a variety of useful things from virtual goods in online games to discounts on insurance premiums.

Blacksburg Coder School


Blacksburg Coder School is an organization that provides a safe, fun, and educational environment for young learners to come and discover the world of computer science. We conduct sessions with a classroom feel, and encourage creativity to flourish. Our qualified instructors are experts in the subject matter because this is what they do for a career. We are now a society where knowing how to code is as important as knowing how to do times tables. We introduce the kids to many different types of programming, including several different languages. This will allow them to really experience the entire spectrum of the coding world.

History Line


History Line is a collaborative learning game for the iPad focusing on US History. The game can be played by 1-6 players. As a single player game, a series of historical events are presented on the screen and the goal of the game is to place the events onto a timeline at the bottom of the screen in the correct sequence. When the event placed on the line is correct the date appears below.

Number Line 2


Number Line 2 is an educational game app to help students learn about fractions, decimals, and percents by ordering equivalent fractions, decimals, and percents on a number line. The app features multiple levels of increasing difficulty where the player must drag circles with either a percent, decimal or fraction onto a number line in the correct sequence. To advance to the next level, players must correctly place all the circles on the number line before time runs out. Players can also choose to play in fractions and decimals mode or only fractions mode.

Puzzlement (*Some Assembly Required)


Puzzlement is a multi-player jigsaw puzzle game for the iPad. Choose from a library of puzzles including a brand new Puzzle of the Day every morning. Partner with your friends on Game Center to complete puzzles as a team, or let Game Center auto-match you with other players around the world. Make puzzles from the photos in your iPad Photo Library and share with your Game Center friends.
